# Mark He Huang

> Computer Science PhD student based in Singapore, working on 3D/4D perception, learning, and reasoning in physical spaces.

## Biography

Hi there, I'm Mark He Huang, currently based in Singapore. I work on 3D/4D perception, learning, and reasoning in physical spaces. I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at [Singapore University of Technology and Design](https://www.sutd.edu.sg/), and my PhD research is generously supported by [A*STAR](https://www.a-star.edu.sg/) Computing and Information Science (ACIS/AGS Computing) Scholarship.
